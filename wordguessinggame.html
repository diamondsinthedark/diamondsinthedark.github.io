How to Play:

There's a secret word. Try to guess the word!

If you guess a correct letter but it's in the wrong spot, it will turn yellow.

If you guess a letter and it's in the right spot, it will turn green!

Keep guessing until all letters are green.

All solutions are found within Glass Animals song lyrics and titles (including covers).

Enjoy!
